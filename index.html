Questions

Everything you want to know
before you start.

Do my suppliers need to do anything?
For suppliers with existing ERP systems, we connect via standard API. For smaller suppliers, we provide a simple self-service portal to manage their catalogue — no IT involvement needed. We handle the integration for you.
How long does it take to get set up?
Most kitchens are placing their first real order within 30 minutes. We onboard you with a short video call, connect your existing suppliers, and you're live. No technical setup required on your side.
Does voice ordering work in a noisy kitchen?
Yes — we've specifically tuned ChefCompras for kitchen environments. There's always a typed fallback if it's too loud, and you can correct any item before confirming. The system also learns your usual items and shorthand over time.
Can I keep my existing suppliers?
Absolutely. ChefCompras works alongside your current suppliers and simply adds price visibility on top. You can set preferred-supplier rules so certain items always go to your trusted rep, regardless of price.
What's included in the 3-month free trial?
Everything in the Growth plan — voice ordering, price comparison, smart basket, spend analytics and integrations — completely free for 3 months. No credit card required. At the end you choose whether to continue. No auto-charge.
Is my ordering data kept private?
Yes. Your order data and the prices you see are yours and are never shared with competitors or third parties. Suppliers only see the orders placed with them — not what others are quoting you.
